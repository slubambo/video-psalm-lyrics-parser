I'm Forever Grateful.

You did not wait for me
to draw near to You
But You clothed Yourself
with frail humanity!

You did not wait for me
to cry out to You
But You let me hear
Your Voice calling me!

And I'm Forever Grateful
to You
I'm Forever Grateful
for The Cross
I'm Forever Grateful
to You
That You came
to seek and save the lost.
