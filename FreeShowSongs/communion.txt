communion

We do not come to your table
merciful Lord
Trusting in our own goodness
But in your mercy

We are not good enough
Even to eat the crumbs
That fall from your table

But you never change
Your nature is always
to have mercy

We therefore humbly ask you gracious Lord
To let us eat the flesh of your dear son Jesus Christ

So that our sinful bodies
May be made clean by His body

and our souls washed
By His precious blood

And that we may Forever dwell in Him, And he in us, Amen.
