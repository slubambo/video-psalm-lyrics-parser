You are good – Charity Worship Team 2006

As we worship the Lord
Let his glory abound
As we lift up our voice
Let his presence abound

We have come to say you are Holy, Jesus
We have come to say you are worthy

We have come to say, you are good
We have come to say thank you.

Holy, Holy, Holy,
Holy is the Lord
Worthy, Worthy, Worthy, is the Lamb

Thank you Lord,
you are good.
