Ninsiim'omusharaba

Nkaba ngyenda obutoosha,
Ndemir'ebibi byangye.
Hatein'ekyakubasa kunyih'obusaasi.

Ninsiim'omusharaba,
Ninsiima eky'ekituuro,
Kandi okukira byona,
Ninsiim'owambambirwe.

Kandi kunahikire,
Aha musharaba gwe;
Ahu ntaryeebwa obundi,
Niho nahumuriire.

Niho natandikire,
Okugir'obusingye,
Nih'omutwaaro gwangye,
Gw'ebibi gwazikirwe.

Yesu akajwa eshagama,
Y'ekyambu eky'amagara,
Eyozya emitima,
Ekagyereza kimwe
