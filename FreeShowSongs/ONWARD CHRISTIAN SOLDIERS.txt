ONWARD CHRISTIAN SOLDIERS

Onward Christians Soldiers
Marching as to war
With the cross of Jesus
Going on before

Christ the Royal master
Leads against the foe
Onward in to battle
See his banner go.

ONWARD CHRISTIAN SOLDIERS
MARCHING AS TO WAR WITH THE CROSS OF JESUS
GOING ON BEFORE

At The Sign Of Triumph
Satan's Host Doth Flee
On Then Christian Soldiers
On To Victory

Hell's Foundations Quiver
At The Shout Of Praise
Brothers Lift Your Voices
Loud Your Anthems Raise,

Like Amighty army
Rolls the Church of God
Brothers we are standing
Where the Saints have trod
We are not divided

All one body we
One in hope and doctrine
One in Charity

Onward then ye people
Join the happy band
Come and join this army
To the Promised land

Lift his royal standard
High against the foe
Forward into battle
We his Church will go
