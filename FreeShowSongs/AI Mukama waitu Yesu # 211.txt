AI Mukama waitu Yesu # 211

AI Mukama waitu Yesu
Twena n'tukuhimbisa;
Ahakub' okatufeera,
Yes' Omujuni waitu.

Nkaba nfa kubi munonga,
Ngu nkaabon' obusingye;
Ku naateireho, naaremwa;
Yesu, naakwehongyera.

Ninkuh' omubiri gwangye
N'omutima n'obwengye;
Kand' ebi ntugire byona
Bib' ebyawe, Mujuni.

Ndyaguma ngambire boona
Eby'okujuna kwawe,
Okutari kw'ekicweka,
Oku otuheera busha.

Ka nkusiim', owancungwire
Okanjunira kimwe,
Kand' okaguma nondinda;
Yes', osiimw' obutoosha.
