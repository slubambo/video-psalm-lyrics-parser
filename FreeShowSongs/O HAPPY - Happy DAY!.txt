O HAPPY - Happy DAY!

O happy day, that fixed my choice
On you my Savior and my God;

Well may this grateful heart rejoice
And tell of Christ’s redeeming blood

O happy day, O happy day
When Jesus washed my sins away!

He teaches me to watch and pray
And live rejoicing everyday
(Halleluiah) O happy day, O happy day
When Jesus washed my sins away!

It’s done the great transactions done!
I am my Lord’s and He is mine

He led me and I followed on
Responding to the voice divine
O happy day, O happy day…

Now rest my long divided heart;
In Jesus Christ who loves you rest!

And never from your Lord depart,
Enriched in Him, by Him possessed
O happy day, O happy day…

So God who heard my solemn vow
In daily prayer shall hear my voice
Till in my final breathe I bow

And bless the day that fixed my choice
O happy day, O happy day...
