Bwetulisimbibwa awali Yesu # 410

Bwetulisimbibwa awali Yesu
Tunnyonnyole ebyaffe,
Mukama Alituvunaana ebyo
Bye twonoonye ku nsi.

Bw'alikoowola erinnya lyange (Aleruya)
Nze siritya kumuyitaba;
Kubanga nze yannaza omwoyo (Aleruya)
Mu musaayi gwe.

Nze ndifuna engule entukuvu
Ey'obulokozi
Era nze siriva mu maaso ge
emirembe gyonna.

Fenna abaligenda mu ggulu
Tuliwona ensi eno;
Ebyonoono byaffe biriggwawo
Lwa amanyi ge Yesu
