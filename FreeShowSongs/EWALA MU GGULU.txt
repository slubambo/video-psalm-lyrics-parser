EWALA MU GGULU

EWALA mu ggulu,
Bamalayika be,
Bayimba n'essanyu,
Nga batendereza,
Aleruuya!
Basanyuka
Aleruuya!

Era mu nsi muno,
Katonda akkiriza
Annyimba z'abato,
Bonna abamwagala,
Aleruuya!
Naffe ka tumuyimbire,
Aleruuya!

Yesu mulokozi
Takwegairidde,
Otuyigirize,
Amazima gonna,
Aleruuya!
Tulyoke tukuyimbire,
Aleruuya!.

Ayi Yesu Mukama,
Bunya ekigambo kyo,
Buli ggwanga ery'erinsi,
Likyukire gy'oli,
Aleruuya!
Ebika byonna biyimba,
Aleruuya!
