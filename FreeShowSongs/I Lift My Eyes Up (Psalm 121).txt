I Lift My Eyes Up (Psalm 121)

I lift my eyes up, up to the mountains,
Where does my help come from?
My help comes from You, Maker of heaven,
Creator of the earth.

O how I need You, Lord,
You are my only hope,
You're my only prayer;
So I will wait for You
To come and rescue me,
Come and give me life.

You keep my feet firm, You will not slumber,
Oh Lord You keep me whole.
You're at my right hand, a Shade beside me ,
Preserver of my soul.

O how I need You, Lord,
You are my only hope,
You're my only prayer;
So I will wait for You
To come and rescue me,
Come and give me life.
