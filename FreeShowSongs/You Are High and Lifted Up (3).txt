You Are High and Lifted Up.

There is no glory
in my own wisdom.
There is no power
in my own strength.
There is no might
in my own riches.
But I will boast of
knowing you.

For you are high
and lifted up,
The glory of the nations.
You are high and lifted up,
In all all the earth.
You are high and lifted up,
Creator, Redeemer,
And I will boast of knowing you.
