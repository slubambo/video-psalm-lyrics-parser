SAAYUUNI MU GGULU

SAAYUUNI mu ggulu,
Katonda gy’abeera,
Bwe ndifa, ndigenda,
N’essanuy lingi nnyo,
Katonda wang(e), eyo gy’oli,
Ndituuka dd(i) okukulaba?

Omulokozi gy’ali
Gwe baayigganyanga,
Ne bamalayika,

Bayimba ettendo lye,
Katonda wang(e), eyo gy’oli,
Ndituuka dd(i) okukulaba?

Ab’edda baali eyo,
Baakomekkereza,
E??endo zaabwe, era,

Baalaba Mukama,
Katonda wang(e), eyo gy’oli,
Ndituuka dd(i) okukulaba?

Eri n’abatume,
Gye balabikira,
Bonna abamwesiga,
Nga bakuba ennanga,

Katonda wang(e), eyo gy’oli,
Ndituuka dd(i) okukulaba?

Era abajulirwa,
Abaalangibwa Yesu,
Era abamwesiga,

Eyabalokola,
Katonda wang(e), eyo gy’oli,
Ndituuka dd(i) okukulaba?
