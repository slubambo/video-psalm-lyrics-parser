Enjuba y’omwoyo gwange #12

Enjuba y’omwoyo gwange,
Omulokozi gwenjagala,
Bw’aba nga ajakira, obudde
Tebujja kunzibirira.

Emitawaana egy’ensi
Gireme okusiikiriza
Omusana ogw’amaaso go,
Ne siyinza kukulaba.

Bwe ŋŋenda okwebaka ekiro
Onteeke mu mikono gyo,
Onkuume mu kabi konna,
Oneebase mu mirembe.

Enkya mu ttuntu, n’ekiro,
Era ne mu bifo byonna,
Gwe ayagala abaana bo,
Beeranga nange, tondeka

Oba nga omu gwe watonda
Anyoomye ekisa kyo leero,
Kakaano omutaase oyo,
Mu buyinza bw’omulabe.

Ojjanjabe abalumwa ennyo,
N’abatayinza kwebaka,
Obawonye endwadde zaabwe,
Obagyekko obubi bwonna.

Era bwe tunaazuukuka
Ojje otuwe ekisa kyo,
Nga tetunnaba kukola
Emirimu egya bulijjo
