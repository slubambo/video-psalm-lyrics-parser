YESU KINO KYE NJAGALA. #270(220)

Yesu kino kye njagala
Omwoyo nga gugwo;
Omugonvu n’omuwombeefu
Ogukukkiriza

Era omwoyo oguwulira
Ebbiragiro byonna;
Mukama mw’anaatuulanga
Okugusanyusa.

Omwoyo ogunaazibwa
Omusaayi gwa Yesu;
Ogutabeeramu kibi,
Ogujjudde ekisa.

Omwoyo ogwenkaniddaawo
Ogumpe Katonda;
Oneemale nze kaakati,
Onfugire ddala.
