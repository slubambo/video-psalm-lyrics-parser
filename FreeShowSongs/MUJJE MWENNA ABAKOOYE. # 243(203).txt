MUJJE MWENNA ABAKOOYE. # 243(203)

Mujje mwenna Abakokooye
Nze naabawummuza:
Eddoboozi nga lya kisa

Ery’Omulokozi:
Litutegeeza bw’ali
Ow’ekisa okuwonya
Emwoyo gy’abantu.

Mujje mwenna abakyamye
Nze naabamulisa
Eddoboozi nga lya ssanyu

Mu nzikiza ekutte
Mu nsiko ey’omu nsi
Twali tuwabye nnyo,
Mu kkubo lye yatuzza,
Ffe ne tusanyuka

Mujje mwenna abafudde
Naabawa obulamu:
Eddoboozi lye lya maanyi
Lituzzaamu omwoyo.

Entalo zaffe nzibu;
Omulabe mukambwe;
Amaanyi g’okulwana
Gava eri Yesu

Era anajjanga gye ndi
Nze siimugobenga:
Eddoboozi lya Mukama
Lye litugumya ennyo:

Ekisa ekyenkanaawo
Tewali asaanide,
Ye ye atusembezza
Kale tumwebaze
