Let your living water

Let thy living water
Flow over my soul
Let the Holy Spirit
Come and take control

Of every situation
That has troubled
My mind
All my cares and burdens,
unto you I roll

Jesus…
Father…
Spirit

Come now Holy Spirit
& fill my soul
Hold me in your loving arms

and make me whole
Wipe away all doubt
and fear & take my pride
Hold me in your love
and keep me by your side

Give your life to Jesus
Let him fill your soul
Let him take you in his arms

And make you whole
As you give your life to him
He’ll set you free
You will live and reign with
Him eternally.
