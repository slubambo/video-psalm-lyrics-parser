MUSANYUKE ABALONDE. # 29 (29)

MUSANYUKE abalonde,
Abeesiga Yesu,
Ettabaaza zammwe zaake,
Obudde buzibye,

Mukama waffe alijja,
Kaakano ali kumpi,
Muyimuke mwekuume,
Muleme okwebaka.

Mukoleeze ettabaaza,
Zaake mu nzikiza,
Mulabe obulokozi,
Bwammwe busembedde,

Mukama bamulengera,
Kaakano ali kumpi,
Mugende nga muyimba,
Okumusisinkana.

Abawala a'bamagezi,,
Muyimbe nnyo nnyini,
Ennyimba ez'abamalayika,
Yesu atulindirira,

Mulokozi w'abantu,
Balokole ba Yesu,
Musembere mwenna.

Tukkiriza ggwe Yesu,
Mulokozi waffe,
Yanguwa okujja, olye,3

Obwakabaka bwo,
Katonda atubeerenga,
Okubuuliranga wonna,
Abantu bakusenge,
Balokole bonna.
