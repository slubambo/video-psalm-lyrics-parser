GGWE AKAMWA KANGE, TENDANGA #131

Ggwe akamwa kange, tendanga,
Omubiri gwa Yesu,
N'omusaayi gwe eby'omuwendo,
Ebiva mu Kabaka,
Eyazaalibwa Malyamu,
Ye Mununuzi w'ensi.

Bw'atyo ffe bwe yatuwebwa
Okuzaalibwa mu nsi,
Yagumikiriiza ennaku,
Enyweze obubaka bwe,
Yabonyaabonyezebwa nnyo,
Okutuusa ku kufa.

Ekiro eky'enkomerero
Ng'ali mu batume be,
Yesu yabatuukira nnyo,
Bakwatenga ey'obulamu
Gye yawa n'engalo ze,
Emmere y'omuwendo omungi.

Kigambo yafuuka emmere,
Eriisa abantu bonna,
Omusaayi gwe ye nviinyo,
Ebireeta obulamu,
Bwe tubikkiriza ebyo,
Tumanya okwagala kwe.

Kale ka tudende Yesu,
Ku lw'embaga ye eno,
Mu kifo kyebibi eby'ensi,
Tufune eby'o ggulu,
Essuubi lyaffe libereenga
Mu ye ow'olubeerera.

Omukisa n'ekitiibwa,
Bya Kitaffe N'Omwana,
Obukulu era n'ettendo,
Bibe eri ye bulijjo,
Bitulagenga okwagala,
Kw'oyo omu ne Kitaffe.
