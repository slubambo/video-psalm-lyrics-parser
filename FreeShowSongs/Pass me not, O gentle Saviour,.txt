Pass me not, O gentle Saviour,

Pass me not,
O gentle Saviour,
Hear my humble cry;
While on others
Thou art calling,
Do not pass me by.

Saviour, Saviour,
Hear my humble cry;
While on others
Thou art calling,
Do not pass me by.

Let me at Thy
throne of mercy
Find a sweet relief,
Kneeling there in
deep contrition;
Help my unbelief.

Saviour, Saviour,
Hear my humble cry;
While on others
Thou art calling,
Do not pass me by.

Trusting only in
Thy merit,
Would I seek Thy face;
Heal my wounded,
broken spirit,
Save me by Thy grace.

Saviour, Saviour,
Hear my humble cry;
While on others
Thou art calling,
Do not pass me by.

Thou the Spring of
all my comfort,
More than life to me,
Whom have I on earth
beside Thee?
Whom in heaven
but Thee?

Saviour, Saviour,
Hear my humble cry;
While on others
Thou art calling,
Do not pass me by.
