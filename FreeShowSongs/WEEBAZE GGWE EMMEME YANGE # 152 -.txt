WEEBAZE GGWE EMMEME YANGE # 152:

WEEBAZE ggwe
emmeme yange
Weebaze mwoyo gwange;
Olw' ekitibwa kye ekingi ennyo,

Leeta ettendo lyo lyonna;
Aleruuya, Aleruuya!
Weebaze Katonda wo.

Weebaze ekisa kye kingi
Eri bajjajja ab' edda.
Olw' ekisa kye ekitakoma,

Ekyaliwo edda n'edda.
Aleruuya; Aleruuya!
Weebaze Katonda wo.

Ye kitaffe, atukuuma,
Atuliisa bulijjo
Amanyi obunafu bwa ffe ,

Atuwonya mu kabi .
Aleruuya; Aleruuya!
Weebaze Katonda wo.

Bamalayika be bonna
Bayimba ettendo lye,
''Oyanguwe ojje n' ebire

Olye obwakabaka bwo"
Aleruuya; Aleruuya
Weebaze Katonda wo.
