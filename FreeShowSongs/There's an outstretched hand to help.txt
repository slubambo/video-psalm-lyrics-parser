There's an outstretched hand to help

There's an outstretched hand to help
And to guide to righteousness
Through a dang'rous dessert waste
To the land of blessedness
Even if my foot in this unknown way
Should have wandered off the track
Then He waves His hands
And He beckons me
'Till restored He has led me back
There's an outstretched hand to save
And to guide to righteousness.
