TUSINZA NNYO ERINYA LYO. # 97 (71)

Tusinza nnyo erinya lyo
Ayi Yesu Mukama,
Awamu n'abantu bonna,
Kabaka waffe Yesu, afuga byonna.

Mumwebaze mmwwe abattibwa,
Okubalanga ye;
Musinze eyabafiirira:
Kabaka waffe Yesu, afuga byonna.

Nammwe Bayisirayiri be
Abanunulibwa,
Musinze Omwaana wa Dawudi;
Kabaka waffe Yesu, afuga bvonna.

Abantu abaaasonyiyibwa,
Olw'ekisa kye ye,
Mufukaamirire Yesu:
Kabaka waffe Yesu, afuga byonna.

Ammaawanga gonna ag'ensi
Muwulire Yesu:
Erinnya lye lisinzibwe:
Kabaka waffe Yesu, afuga byonna.

Twagala okutukayo
Mu maaso ga Yesu,
Okumweyanza bulijjo:
Kabaka waffe Yesu, afuga byonna.
