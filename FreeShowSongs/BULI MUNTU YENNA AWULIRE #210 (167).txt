BULI MUNTU YENNA AWULIRE #210 (167)

Buli muntu yenna awulire Ebigambo by'Omulokozi w'ensi, Bunya wonna wonna nti Akkiriza Alirokoka mu ye.

Mujje nno gye ndi
abakooy(e) ennyo
Mulyoke muwumulenga n'essanyu.

Buli muntu ajja
naamwaniriza,
Siimugobere bweru.

Buli muntu yenna asembere
Oluggi Iw'omu ggulu lugguddwaawo,
Yesu ge mazima, lye kkubo lyaffe;
Ekibalwisa kiki?

Buli muntu yenna amwagala
Kye kigambo kya
Katonda atalimba;
Kale bannange, tubunye ettendo lye,
Atugabidde buwa.
