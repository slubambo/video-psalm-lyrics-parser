What a lovely Name

What a lovely Name
The Name of Jesus
Reaching high and far
Than the brightest star
For it is sweeter
Than the songs they sing in heaven
Let the world proclaim
What a lovely Name.

He made the stars to shine
He made the rolling sea
He made the mountains high
And He made me
And this is why I love Him
For me He bled and died
The Lord of all creations
Became the crucified.
