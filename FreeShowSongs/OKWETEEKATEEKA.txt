KATONDA Ayinza - byonna, Kitaawe wa Mukama waffe Yesu Kristo, Omutonzi w'ebintu byonna, asala omusango gw'abantu bonna;

Twatula tunakuwalira ebibi byaffe n'okwonoona kwaffe okungi bwe twakakoze obubi ennyo mu bigambo bye twakalowoozezza,

Ne bye twakoogedde, ne bye twakakoze, nga tunyooma ekitiibwa ky'Obwakatonda bwo: kye tuvudde tusaanira ennyo obusungu bwo n'e kiruyi kyo.

Twenenyerezza ddala,tunakuwadde nnyo olw'ebibi byaffe ebyo; bwe tubijjukira emyoyo gyaffe gituluma; obuzito bwabyo butulemye.

Otuddiremu, otuddiremu, Ayi Kitaffe ow'ekisa kyonna: ku bw'Omwana wo Mukama waffe Yesu Kristo, otusonyiwe byonna bye twakakoze,

Otuwe omukisa tukuweerezenga, tukusanyusenga mu mpisa empya kaakano n'ennaku zonna. Erinnya lyo ligulumizibwe, litenderezebwe; ku bwa Yesu Kristo Mukama waffe.
