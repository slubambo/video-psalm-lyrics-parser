YESU MUSUMBA WANGE # 361 (287)

Yesu Musumba wange,
Bulijjo ansanyusa;
Yesu anjagala, ammanyi,

Byonna ebirungi abimpa;
Ampita erinnya lyange,
Ambeera n'ekisa kye.

Ntambula nga sikyatya,
Yesu Mukuumi wange;
Mu biro eby'enjala andiisa,

Era bwe nnumwa ennyonta,
Antwala ku mabbali Ag'amazi amateefu.

Si kirungi nsanyuke?
Si kirungi njaguze?
Ne bwe ndifa talindeka

Alintuusa ne mu ggulu,
Eyo gye naabeeranga,
Mu maaso ga Katonda.
