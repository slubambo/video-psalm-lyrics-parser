YESU ALIJJA N'EBIRE. # 28 (28)

YESU alijja n'ebire,
Eyafiirira abantu,

Abatukuvu be bangi,
Wamu naye bayimba,
Aleruuya!... x3
Ajja wansi okufuga.

Bonna baliraba Yesu,
Ng'ali mu kitiibwa kye:
N'abo abamuyigganya,

N'abo Abamufumita,
Balikaaba …x3
Yesu bw'alirabiika.

Wansi ne waggulu wonna,
N'ebirimu biriggwaawo,
Abaamulyamu olukwe,

Baliwulira Yesu,
Bw'alisala… x3
Gubasinze, muveewo.

Abeegendereza bonna,
Abamulindirira,
Balitwalibwa mu bbanga,

Balisangayo Yesu,
Eyeewaayo … x3
Okuleeta obulamu.

Bonna bonna bakusinza,
Yesu, mu kitiibwa kyo;

Jjangu, Mulokozi waffe,
Olye obwakabaka bwo,
Tukusuute … x3
Jjangu, Mukama waffe.
