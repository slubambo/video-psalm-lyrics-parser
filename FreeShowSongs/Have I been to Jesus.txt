Have I been to Jesus

Have I been to Jesus
for the cleansing power?
Am I washed in the blood of the Lamb?

Am I fully trusting
in his grace each hour?
Am I washed in the blood of the Lamb?

Wash me Lord in the blood,
In the soul-cleansing blood of the Lamb?

Make my garments spotless?
Make them white as snow?
Wash me Lord in the blood of the Lamb.

Am I walking daily
by the Saviour's side?
Am I washed in the blood of the Lamb?

Do I rest each moment in the crucified?
Am I washed in the blood of the Lamb?

When the Bridegroom cometh
will my robes be white,
Pure and white in the blood of the Lamb?

Will my soul be ready
for the mansions bright,
And be washed in the blood of the Lamb?

I will Lay aside the garments
that are stained with sin,
And be washed in the blood of the Lamb!

There's a fountain flowing
for the soul unclean;
I'll, be washed in the blood of the Lamb!
