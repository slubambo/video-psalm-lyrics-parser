Tulina Kabaka waffe # 358

Tulina Kabaka waffe
Eyava mu ggulu:
Kale fenna abaana be
Tumugoberere.

Okwagala kwe kunji nnyo
Eri abantu bonna;
Naffe kwagale bannaffe
Nga bwe kiyinzika.

Yesu tayagala kibi
Nakwerowoozako;
Ye omulaba we y'ani
Amulyamu olukwe?

Ayagala tulongoose
Emibiri gyaffe;
Abatali batukuvu
Tebaliba naye

Tuli kibiina kitono,
Naye tusaanidde
Okutwalira Katonda
Emyoyo emirongoofu.
