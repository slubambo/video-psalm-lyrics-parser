Your Steadfast Love

Your Steadfast Love
Extends To The Heavens
Your Faithfulness
Reaches To The Sky
Your Righteousness
Is Like Majestic Mountains
And Your Wisdom
Like The Depth Of The Sea
And You Come To Me

Filling My Heart
With Your Loving Kindness
I Find My Peace
In The Shadow Of Your Wings
I Eat My Fill
From The Abundance Of Your Household
And I Drink
From The Streams Of Rejoicing
You Are My King
