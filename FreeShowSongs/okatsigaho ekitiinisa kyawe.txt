okatsigaho ekitiinisa kyawe;
Obu waija kutucungura;
Kwonk'obu waazaarirwa Betelehemu,
Toratungire gye mwanya

Yes',ij' omu mutima gwangye Naagukuha,
Mukama wangye Ij'otaah'
omu mutima gwangye,
Omwanya gwawe gurimu.

Baamaraika bakaba bakuramya
Obu waabair' ori omu iguru;
Kwonk' obu waazaarw' omu ns',
okeecureeza Waazaarirw' omu nju y'ente.

Yes',ija,n'ebindi
Enyonyi zoona zigir' ah'okwarika
Na zaamuha zigir' amaaya Kwonk'
Iw' okab' obur' ah' okut' omutwe,
Ei waabair' oza yoona
Yes',ija, n'ebindi

Kand' ekyakureetsire n'okwih' abantu
Omu buhuuku bwa sitaane;
Kwonka bo bakakwanga ,
baakwita kubi Nibakubamb' aha muti.

Yes',ija,n'ebindi
