We will wait upon the Lord

We will run and not grow weary We will walk and will not faint
For the Lord will go before us And his joy will be our strength

Mounting up with wings like eagles
As the spirit start to soar When we come into His presence
And we wait upon the Lord

We will wait upon the Lord
For in His presence there’s fullness of joy
And our strength will be restored
As we wait upon the Lord
