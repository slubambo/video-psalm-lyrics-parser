YESU NG'ADDAYO GYE YAVA #101

YESU ng'addayo gye yava,
Mu buwanguzi obungi,
Mumulabe ye Kabaka
Afuga bakabaka,

Bamalayika be bonna,
Bakuba nnyo ennanga,
Eggye lyonna er'yomu ggulu,
Baaniriza Katonda.

Ani ajja kitiibwa,
N'obuwanguzi obungi,
Oyo ow'amaanyi mu ntalo,
Awangudde omulabe,

Bwe yafa ku musaalaba,
Era n'ava mu ntaana,
N'aweebwa erinnya erisinga,
Bonna bamusinzenga.

Atuzuukizza naffe,
Tufuge wamu naye,
Eyo mu kitiibwa ekingi,
Ne bamalayika be,

Omulokozi afuga,
Katonda era omuntu,
Erinnya lye wa kitalo,
Katonda ataggwaawo.

Ekitiibwa eri Kitaffe,
N'eri Omwana we Yesu,
N'eri Omwoyo Omutukuvu,
Omu mu Busatu,

Ekitiibwa kibe gy'ali,
Ensi zimuyimbire,
Ettendo libe mu ggulu,
N'emirembe ku nsi.
