Wedding 222

Percel, will you take Brenda Natukunda
to be your wife, will you love her,

comfort her, honour her and protect her
and forsaking all others, be faithful to her
as long you both shall live?

Brenda, will you take Percel Habaasa to
be your husband, will you love him,

comfort him, honour him and protect
him and forsaking all others, be faithful
to him as long you both shall live?
