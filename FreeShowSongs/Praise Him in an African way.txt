Praise Him in an African way

When Jesus came down; He came down from Heaven
When He landed; He landed in Israel

But when there was trouble; He came down to Africa
So we must Praise Him; Praise Him in an African way

There was a King whose name was Herod
He wanted to kill all the children

But then Jesus came down; He came down to Africa
So we must praise Him; Praise Him in an African way

None one could touch Him, ’cause He is the Son of God
No one could kill Him, ’cause He is the King of Kings

His name is Jesus, Jesus the Savior
So we must praise Him; Praise Him in an African way
