OKWAGALA OKUTAGGWAWO. #172 (122)

Okwagala
okutaggwawo
Ok'wolubeerera,
Kukulukuta n'amanyi,
Ge mazzi agataggwawo.

Ffe tetuyinza kulinya,
Mukama Kristo akke,
Ye abeera wonna - wonna,
Tewali w'abula.

Naye ye Mubeezi waffe,
Atubeera bulijjo,
Alabwa mu kizikiza,
Ne mu kwagala era.

Ebyambalo bye byawonya,
Endwadde z'abantu,
Tumweyune bwe tulumwa,
Tuwonere ddala.

Okwagala okutagambika,
Erinnya lyo liwonya,
Okukugaana kwe kufa,
Ggwo olina obulamu.
