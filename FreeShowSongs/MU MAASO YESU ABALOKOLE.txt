MU MAASO YESU ABALOKOLE.

Mu maaso ga Yesu abalokole,
Ekibiina ekinene, bayimiridde,
Bayimba nti eyatunaaza yekka,
Aweebwe ekitiibwa ennaku zonna.

Tumuwe, Tumuwe
Ekitiibwa Yesu
Tuyimbe, Tuyimbe,
Ensi esanyuke
Mujje eri Kitaffe n’Omwana Yesu,
Aweebwe ekitiibwa
Emirembe gyonna.

Kiki ekyabanaaza balina ebibi,
Yesu yatunazza n'omusaayi bw'ati,
Tuyimba nti eyatunaaza yekka,
Aweebwe ekitiibwa ennaku zonna.

Tumuwe, Tumuwe
Ekitiibwa Yesu
Tuyimbe, Tuyimbe,
Ensi esanyuke
Mujje eri Kitaffe n’Omwana Yesu,
Aweebwe ekitiibwa
Emirembe gyonna.

Kyebava
bafaanana batukuvu,
Kyebava bayimba abangi wamu,
Ggwe Yesu wekka eyatutukuza,
Oweebwe ekitiibwa ennaku zonna.

Bo baali bajeemu, naye kaakano,
Be baddu ba Yesu abamuweereza
bayimba nti eyatunaaza yekka,
Aweebwe ekitiibwa ennaku zonna.

Yesu, ffe fenna twandifudde bufi,
Watwagala ne
bwe twali ababi,
N'otusaasira ggwe n'otutukuza,
Oweebwe ekitiibwa ennaku zonna.

Bannange,
n'essanyu tumuyimbire,
Abalala balyoke bayimbe nabo,
Ggwe Yesu wekka eyatutukuza,
Oweebwe ekitiibwa ennaku zonna.
