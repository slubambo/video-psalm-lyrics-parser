YESU YAJJA ALOKOLE ABALINA EBIBI # 289 (228)

Yesu yajja alokole Abalina ebibi:
Naffe fenna aboonoonyi
Anaatusonyiwa.

Neeyanz(e) nnoYesu
ku lw’okwagal(a) okunene
Ne ndaba essanyu lingi nnyo,
Kubanga ndokose.

Tegwatuyiikira omusaayi
Ogusinga byonna?
Tewali gw'atayinza ye
Okulongoosa ennyo.

Tetuyinza ffe okwetuusa
Mu ggulu n'akamu:
Mu kifo ekyo mulimuka
Abatuukirivu.

Naye bwe tumukkiriza
Katonda Kitaffe
Atulaba ng'abalina
Obutulikirivu.

Mukale mumukkirize
Muweebwe obulamu:
Kubanga Yesu tagoba
Mu maaso ge n'omu.
