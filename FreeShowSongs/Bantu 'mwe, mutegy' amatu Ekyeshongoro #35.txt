Bantu 'mwe, mutegy' amatu Ekyeshongoro #35

Bantu 'mwe, mutegy' amatu Ekyeshongoro
kyaitu Eky'okuhimbisa Yesu Omwana wa Ruhanga.
Akazaarwa nk'er' izooba Omwishiki oshugaine,

Baamairaka baabuzya Amakuru g'okuzaarwa kwe.
Twena ka tuhimbise
Yes' ozairw' ari Omukama.

Krist' osiimw' ab' omu iguru, Nyakusinga otahwaho Waikiriza kwecureeza N'okuzaarw' omwishiki Ori Ruhanga buzima, Kand' ori omuntu

Nyabuntu, Waija kutuura naitwe Nooyetw' eiziin' Emmanueli. Twena ka tuhimbise,n'ebindi

Ai Mukama w'obusingye Okarug' omu iguru, Waikiriza kutsigaho Ekitiinisa kyawe.

Ahabw'okukund' abantu Ngu obone kutujuna Omu bibi byaitu byona. Twena ka tuhimbise,n'ebindi

Iw'ori Omushana gw'ensi Gw'okujwer' amahanga, Ngu garugy' omu mwirima, Gaije gakwehongere.

Obusingye bub' omu nsi Ahabw'okuzaarwa kwawe; Mbwenu ka tukuramye, Yes' Omucunguzi waitu. Twena ka tuhimbise, n'ebindi
