I dyewor me can ki lworo #163

I dyewor me can ki lworo
Wan wakwanyo yo mewa,
Wan wa lwak pa Rwotwa Yecu,
Wan wageno cik mere.

Mac ma ryeny me telo wiwa,
Menyo wa i piny macol;
Wan wamako ciŋ luwotwa,
Pe walworo gin marac.

Mac acel pa Rwot kikome,
Menyo lwak mere ducu.
En biryemo can ki lworo,
Miyo yo nibedo leŋ.

Tam acel otero ducu,
Wan waribbe pi niye;
Wan waneno Yecu kene,
En ladit mewa acel.

Jo mapol ma wel pe kwanne,
Gin giwero dwon acel,
Lweny acel ma gin gilwenyo,
Wot acel ki yo acel.

Yom-cwiny ka watuno kunnu
Loka nam, yom-cwiny acel;
Rwot ma kunnu En e Wonwa,
Wan litinone ducu.

Eno ba, litino tuwa,
Yatariya telo yo,
Pe walwor nilubo kore,
Koŋ walwenyo, dok waywe.

Kare cok ma gibicoyo
Jo muto nia ki lyel;
Ci wan kwedgi wabinene,
Can ki colpiny doŋ bikee.
