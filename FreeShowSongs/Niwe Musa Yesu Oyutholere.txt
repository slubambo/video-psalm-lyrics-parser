Niwe Musa Yesu Oyutholere

Niwe Musa Yesu
Oyutholere

Ghuli mwami wolhwanzu lhunene
(You are the king of so much Love)
Muwalhukangya Egologotha
(You showed this at Golgotha)

Mughutha Omusasi waghu habwingye (You shed your blood for me)
Nangabana erilhama (So I can be saved)

Yesu Kristo sithuwithe ekyathwangakuha (Jesus Christ, we don’t have what to offer you)
Erilhusya ku erikwimbira (Apart from singing for you)

N’eripipa erina lyaghu (And praising your holy name)
Habwa ebibuya ghukakolha (For the beautiful things you do)

Omusasi Owuthika ekaluvari (For the blood that was shed at Calvary)
Ingye ngabana obwiranda (I find freedom)

Ebyosi ebyawalhabamu ningye
(All the pain you went through)
Nangabere inabilhabamu
(It would have been me going through that)

You deserve more than I can give
My saviour on the cross
We praise you we worship you
All praise and glory to his name
