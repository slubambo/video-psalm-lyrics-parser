Lwananga Mu lutalo lwo 216 (147)

Lwananga mu lutalolwo,
Okulwana okulungi,
Nyweza ggwe obulamu bwo,
Yesu atuwa empeera.

Wakana ggwe omuwakanyi,
Leeta amaanyi go gonna,
Gumira olugendolwo,
Yesu atuwa empeera

Leka okwelarikirira,
Tulina Omusaale waffe,
Mwesige ye oliraba
Yesu bw’atuwa ebirungi

Tozirika, ali kumpi,
Tajjulula kwagala kwe,
Kkiriza ggwe, oliraba
Yesu bw’ali Omulokozi
