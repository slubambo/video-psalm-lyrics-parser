Nsanyukira ekigambo kino
Yesu Mukama atwagala nnyo

Alina bingi ebisanyusa
Naye ekisinga nti nze kwagala

Anjagala, anjagala;
Nga kitalo (o)kunjagala

Angal(a) olw’ekisa kye,
Nange era mwagala

Bwe mba nyonoona ne mwerabira
Yesu tandeka kubula ddala

Ampita mangu okumweyuna ye
Era anjijukiza okwakagla kwe

Bwe nditunuulila ekitiibwa kyo
Ndiyimba ntya, bwe siyimba bwe ntyo?

Nnyimba emirembe n’emirembe
Yesu kiki ekyakunjagaza nze

Anjagala, nange mwagala ye:
Yakka mu nsi olw’okagala kwe;

Kwagala kwamuleeta ku muti;
Yesu atwagala ffe bwagazi
