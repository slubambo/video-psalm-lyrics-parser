ENDAGAANO ENTUKUVU #203 (144)

ENDAGAANO entukuvu,
Gye njagalira ddala,
Byonna bye neetaaga nze,
Birabikira mu yo.

Omwoyo Omukukuvu,
Yagiwandiisa edda,
Teyagunjibwa bantu,
Kyenaavanga ngyesiga.

Bwe nkyama enkomyawo,
Bwemba ngwa ennyimusa,
Bwe siraba mu kkubo,
Eba gye ndi omusana.

Mu nnaku ensanyusa,
Yesu ng’anjigiriza,
Era ewoomesa gye ndi,
Obulamu, n’okufa.
