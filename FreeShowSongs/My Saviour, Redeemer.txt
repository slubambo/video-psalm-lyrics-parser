My Saviour, Redeemer

My Saviour, Redeemer
Lifted me from
the miry clay

Almighty, Forever,
I’ll never
be the same

‘Cause You came here
From the everlasting
To the world we live
The Father’s only Son

You lived, You died
You rose again on high
You opened the way
for the world to live again
Hallelujah,
For all You’ve done
