My Deliverer

,,,,,,,

Joseph took his wife and a child and went to Africa.
To escape the rage of a deadly king.
There along the banks of the Nile. Jesus listened to the song,
That the captive children used to sing. They were singing:

My Deliverer is coming, my Deliverer is standing by.
My Deliverer is coming, my Deliverer is standing by.
,,,,,,,

Through a dry and thirsty land, water from the Kenyan heights.
Pours itself out of Lake Sangri’s broken heart.
There in the Sahara winds, Jesus heard the whole world cry ,
For the healing that would flow from His own scars. The world was singing:

He will never break His promise, He has written it upon the sky.

I will never doubt His promise, if I doubt my heart, I doubt my eyes.

He will never break His promise, though the stars should break within the sky.
