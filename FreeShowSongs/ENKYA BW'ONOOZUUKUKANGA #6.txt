ENKYA BW'ONOOZUUKUKANGA #6

Enkya bw'onoozuukukanga,
Nga tosoose mirimu,
Sooka osome mu kitabo,
Yesu kye yawandiisa,

N'ofukamira mu maaso,
Ga Katonda Omulamu,
Anaakuwanga omukisa,
Bwomubuulira byonna.

Oba ssanyu, oba nnaku,
Bwe bijja gy'oli leero;
Tebiireme kukusanga,
Nga weeteeseteese nyo.

Totya maanyi ga mulabe,
Ssetaani muwangule,
Onoomukubanga enfuka;
Bw'onokkiriza Yesu.

Ebigambo bya Katonda,
Bikulisa omwoyo gwo,
Nga bw'obyekkaanya era bw'otyo,
Bw'olaba emirembe gye.

Mu kusaba mw’onooweerwa,
Amagezi n'amaanyi,
Okugoba abalabe,
Bwe bakulumba obubi.

Obukoowu n'obunafu,
Era n'obuyinike,
N'emitego gya Ssetaani,
Onoosobola byonna.

Saba Yesu okukubeera,
Buli lw’olaba ennaku;
Bw'otyo bw'onoofuna amaanyi,
Bw'omubuulira byonna.
